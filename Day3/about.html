About This Website

This website allows you to check the weather around you, so you can go out without any fear of unexpected conditions. You can view real-time updates, temperature, humidity, and rainfall predictions to help you plan your day better. Whether you're going for a walk, a long trip, or just stepping outside, this site keeps you prepared. With accurate forecasts and an easy-to-use interface, you’ll always know what to expect before you leave home.
